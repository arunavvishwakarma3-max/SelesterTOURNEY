🎯 Tier Rankings

Official MCPE PvP tier system — get evaluated and climb the ranks.

👑
HT1

Hyper Tier 1 — Elite

The absolute top tier. Players in HT1 are the best of the best — godlike PvP mechanics, flawless game sense, and tournament winners. Less than 1% of players.

⭐
LT1

Low Tier 1 — Elite

Still elite level. Slightly below HT1 but can compete at the highest levels. Strong mechanical skill and game knowledge. Top 3% of players.

⚔️
HT2

Hyper Tier 2 — Advanced

Advanced players with excellent PvP skills. Can hold their own in most fights. Good understanding of strats and game mechanics. Top 15%.

🔥
LT2

Low Tier 2 — Advanced

Solid advanced players. Above-average mechanics and consistent performance. Still developing toward elite. Strong competitors.

💪
HT3

Hyper Tier 3 — Intermediate

Intermediate players with decent PvP skills. Knows the basics well and improving. Can win fights but still inconsistent at times.

⚡
LT3

Low Tier 3 — Intermediate

Developing intermediate. Has the basics down but still learning advanced mechanics. Shows promise with practice.

🌱
HT4

Hyper Tier 4 — Rookie

Rookie level. Has basic understanding of PvP but lacks experience. Lots of room for growth — consistent practice will move you up.

🌿
LT4

Low Tier 4 — Rookie

Entry rookie. Still learning the basics of PvP. Everyone starts here — keep practicing and you'll climb the ranks!

📈
Below Avg

Below Average — Rising

New to MCPE PvP. Still developing fundamental skills. With dedication and practice, you'll reach the next tier in no time.

📋 How Tier Testing Works

  1. Go to the #tier-test channel in the Discord server
  2. Select your gamemode (Skywars, BUHC, FUHC, Boxing, Midfight, Bedfight)
  3. A ticket is created — a staff member will claim it
  4. Play your evaluation match against the tester
  5. Staff submits the result using /tier result
  6. Your tier is announced in #tier-results

Results are final. Tests are conducted fairly by certified tier testers.
