What is Celestia? ▼
Celestia is a premium multi-purpose Discord bot built for MCPE PvP servers. It features tournament management, tier testing, ranked matchmaking, music playback, server moderation, tickets, suggestions, giveaways, staff applications, auto-roles, and more — all with professional premium-styled embeds.
How do I set up the bot? ▼
Every system has a /setup command that auto-creates all needed channels and categories. Just run /tier setup, /ranked setup, /music setup, /welcome setup, etc. — one command per system and you're ready to go.
How does tier testing work? ▼
Go to #tier-test, select your gamemode (Skywars, BUHC, FUHC, Boxing, Midfight, or Bedfight). A ticket is created for a staff member to claim. You play your evaluation match, and the staff member submits your tier result using /tier result. Your new tier is posted in #tier-results.
What are the tier rankings? ▼
From highest to lowest: HT1 (Elite), LT1 (Elite), HT2 (Advanced), LT2 (Advanced), HT3 (Intermediate), LT3 (Intermediate), HT4 (Rookie), LT4 (Rookie), Below Avg (Rising). Check the Tiers page for full details.
How do I play music? ▼
Join any voice channel and use /music playmusic <song name>. The bot will join your VC and play audio from YouTube. Use /music queue to see upcoming songs, /music skip to skip, /music volume to adjust, and /music loop to enable queue looping.
How do I apply for staff? ▼
Use /apply staff and fill out the modal with your IGN, age, why you want staff, your experience, and your available hours. The application is posted in #staff-applications where staff can review and react.
Can I get auto-roles when new members join? ▼
Yes! Use /autorole add <role> to add a role that will be automatically assigned to every new member. You can add multiple roles. Use /autorole list to see current auto-roles and /autorole remove to remove one.
How do I create suggestions? ▼
Use /suggestion submit <your idea> or /suggest submit <your idea>. Staff can approve or deny using /suggestion approve and /suggestion deny. Members can vote with ✅ and ❌ reactions on the suggestion message.
How do tournaments work? ▼
Run /t setup once to create roles and channels. Then /t create to start a tournament. Players register with /t enter. Staff start check-in with /t start. After check-in ends with /t checkin_end, brackets generate automatically. Results are submitted with /t result.
The bot is not responding to commands. What do I do? ▼
Make sure the bot has the necessary permissions (view channels, send messages, embed links, manage channels for setup commands). Try mentioning the bot @Celestia to see if it responds. If still stuck, ask an admin to restart the bot.
